# About the Forum

We believe there is a significant chance transformative AI will be developed in the next few years. Securing those systems is on the critical path to preventing global catastrophe — and to ensuring the benefits of powerful AI are broadly realized.

Vegas AI Security Forum '26 brings together security engineers and researchers, policymakers, and members of the national security community to drive thought partnership and advance the field of AI security.

# Format

  • → Application-based attendance, ~200 attendees
  • → Keynote talks, workshops, and fireside chats
  • → Panels, tabletop exercises, and meals
  • → Runs alongside DEF CON week
