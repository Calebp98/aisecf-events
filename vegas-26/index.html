# 2026 Theme: Trust No Model

AI systems are now infrastructure. They route decisions, sign code, manage agents, and increasingly act autonomously. The attack surface has never been larger — and most defenders haven't caught up.

VASF 2026 brings together the researchers who are actively breaking and hardening these systems: red teamers at frontier labs, academic adversarial ML researchers, government threat analysts, and independent security engineers.

# Format

  • → Invitation-only, capped at 70 attendees
  • → Chatham House rules by default
  • → Technical depth over marketing fluff
  • → Evening CTF: live red team exercises
