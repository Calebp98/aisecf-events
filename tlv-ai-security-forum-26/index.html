# About the Forum

We believe there is a significant chance transformative AI will be developed in the next few years. Securing those systems is on the critical path to preventing global catastrophe — and to ensuring the benefits of powerful AI are broadly realized.

Tel Aviv AI Security Forum '26 brings together security engineers and researchers, policymakers, entrepreneurs, and members of the national security community to drive thought partnership and advance the field of AI security.

The Forum is presented by Heron — a gateway into the local AI security community and its programs.

# Format

  • → Application-based attendance
  • → Keynote talks, workshops, and fireside chats
  • → Parallel seminar rooms, one-on-ones, and meals
  • → A full day, ending with dinner at ~8 PM

  • → Two parallel seminar rooms (~45 seats each)
